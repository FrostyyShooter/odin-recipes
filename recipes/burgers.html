Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun
  • Hamburger

Steps

  1. Cut the bun in half
  2. Cut the tomato and unions in slices
  3. Rinse the lettuce and dry them off
  4. Add some mayo and ketchup to the bottom half of the bun
  5. Bake the burger
  6. Put the burger on the bun
  7. Put a slice of cheese on the burger
  8. Put some union slices on the cheese
  9. Put some lettuce on the union slices
  10. Put the tomato slices on it
  11. Add the top of the bun on it
